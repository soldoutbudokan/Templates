# Beyond Meat Quesadillas

Crispy tortillas with melted cheese and seasoned Beyond Meat patties, finished with chilli oil.

**Servings:** 1 per patty (scales linearly)

## Ingredients (per quesadilla)

- 1 Beyond Meat patty
- 2 medium flour tortillas
- Shredded cheese (enough to cover one side of the patty + one tortilla)
- Chilli oil
- Vegetable oil
- Salt

## Instructions

1. Place a Beyond Meat patty between two sheets of parchment paper and flatten until it's roughly the same diameter as the tortillas. Salt one side only.

2. Spread chilli oil on one side of each tortilla. Set aside.

3. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat until the oil shimmers.

4. Place the flattened patty in the pan. Cook until the first side is done, then flip. Immediately add cheese to the cooked side. Once the second side is cooked and the cheese is melting, remove and set aside.

5. Place a tortilla in the pan, chilli oil side down. Add cheese on top, then the Beyond Meat patty (cheese side facing up), then the second tortilla (chilli oil side facing up).

6. Cook until the bottom tortilla is crisp, then flip the whole quesadilla. Cook until the second side is crisp.

7. Remove from pan, slice, and serve. Repeat for remaining patties.
