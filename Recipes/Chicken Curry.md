# Chicken Curry

A warmly spiced, creamy chicken curry with a tomato and onion base — comforting and easy to make. Built around a full 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes.

**Prep Time:** 20 minutes (plus 30 minutes to overnight marinating)
**Cook Time:** 45 minutes
**Servings:** 8

## Ingredients

### For the Marinade

- 800 g boneless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into chunks
- ¾ cup Greek yogurt
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- ½ tbsp ginger, grated (or ginger paste)
- ½ tsp turmeric
- ½ tsp chili powder
- ½ tsp ground cumin
- ½ tsp ground coriander
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp cooking oil
- 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)

### For the Curry

- 2 large onions, thinly sliced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, grated (or ginger paste)
- 800 g canned crushed tomatoes (28 oz can)
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2½ tbsp curry powder
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- 1 tsp ground coriander
- ¼ tsp turmeric
- ¼ tsp chili powder (adjust to taste)
- 1 tsp salt
- 3 tbsp cooking oil
- 1 tsp whole cumin seeds
- 1 tsp black or brown mustard seeds
- 1 bay leaf or 8–10 curry leaves
- ½ cup water
- 3 tbsp fresh cilantro, chopped

### For Serving

- Steamed rice — about 3 cups (≈ 550 g) uncooked for 8 servings (includes a ~10% buffer), or naan

## Instructions

1. **Marinate the chicken.** In a bowl, stir together the Greek yogurt, garlic, ginger, turmeric, chili powder, cumin, coriander, salt, oil, and lemon juice. Add the chicken and coat well. Cover and marinate at least 30 minutes, or up to overnight in the fridge.

2. **Cook the chicken.** Heat 1–2 tbsp of the oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add the marinated chicken, letting excess marinade drip off, and cook until browned and mostly cooked through, 6–8 minutes. The yogurt may curdle slightly and leave browned bits — that's fine and adds flavor. Remove the chicken and set aside.

3. **Temper spices & sauté onions.** Add the remaining oil to the same pot. Add the cumin seeds, mustard seeds, and bay or curry leaves and let them sizzle for 30–60 seconds until fragrant and the mustard seeds start to pop. Add the sliced onions and cook until soft and golden, 8–10 minutes.

4. **Bloom spices.** Add the garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute. Stir in the curry powder, cumin, coriander, turmeric, and chili powder, toasting 30 seconds until fragrant.

5. **Simmer the sauce.** Pour in the crushed tomatoes and water, scraping up any browned bits. Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.

6. **Return the chicken.** Add the cooked chicken and any resting juices back to the pot. Simmer uncovered 8–10 minutes until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens.

7. **Finish.** Stir in the heavy cream and simmer 5 minutes. Garnish with cilantro and serve over rice or with naan.
