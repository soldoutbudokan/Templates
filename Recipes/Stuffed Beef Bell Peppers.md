# Stuffed Beef Bell Peppers

Bell pepper halves filled with seasoned ground beef, rice, and tomato, baked until tender and cheesy. Built around a full 28 oz can of crushed tomatoes.

**Prep Time:** 20 minutes
**Cook Time:** 40 minutes
**Servings:** 8

## Ingredients

- 8 large bell peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
- 900 g ground beef
- ¾ cup uncooked white rice (≈ 2¼ cups cooked)
- 1 large onion, diced
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 800 g canned crushed tomatoes (28 oz can)
- ½ can (3 oz / 85 g) tomato paste (a full 6 oz can also works)
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1½ tsp Italian seasoning
- 1¼ tsp salt
- ½ tsp black pepper
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella or cheddar
- 1½ tbsp olive oil

## Instructions

1. **Cook the rice.** Cook the ¾ cup uncooked rice (it yields about 2¼ cups cooked). Day-old or leftover rice works best for texture.

2. **Preheat.** Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F), or an air fryer to 180°C (360°F). Lightly grease a baking dish (or line the air fryer basket).

3. **Prep the peppers.** Cut each pepper in half lengthwise through the stem and remove the seeds and membranes. Halving keeps them stable cut-side up — there's no need to par-boil or blanch them first.

4. **Brown the beef.** Heat the olive oil in a large skillet. Cook the onion until soft, add the garlic for 30 seconds, then add the beef and brown, breaking it up. Drain excess fat if needed.

5. **Build the filling.** Stir in about three-quarters of the crushed tomatoes, the tomato paste, soy sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer 5 minutes, then mix in the cooked rice and half the cheese.

6. **Stuff.** Arrange the pepper halves cut-side up and spoon in the filling. Top with the remaining crushed tomatoes and cheese.

7. **Bake.** *Oven:* cover with foil and bake 30 minutes, then uncover and bake 10 more until the peppers are tender and the cheese is bubbly. *Air fryer:* cook at 180°C (360°F) for 15–18 minutes until tender and bubbly (no foil needed; work in batches if they won't fit in one layer).
